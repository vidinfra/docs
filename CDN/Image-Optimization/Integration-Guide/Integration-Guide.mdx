These guides explain how to integrate Tenbyte services with your website or applications, depending on the platform you use.

## How It Works
- You define a list of allowed image widths for your project.
- The image component always rounds requests to the nearest allowed width.
- Images are delivered from your Tenbyte CDN subdomain, optimized on-the-fly.
